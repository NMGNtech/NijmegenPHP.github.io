Next Meet-Up: Wednesday, February 11, 2015

After a pretty succesful first meetup in January, it's time to meet up again with your fellow PHP developers from the Nijmegen area. Have a drink, a slice of pizza and a great presentation by Rick Kuipers!

Stateful ElePHPants by Rick Kuipers

Talks are so 2014, join me for some live coding! We'll be broadening our horizons by having a look at state machines and the state pattern. I'll be showing you that these concepts have their place in the PHP world and during the live coding we can share some of our favorite PhpStorm features/shortcuts. Oh yeah, we wil be observing an elephpant in its natural habitat...
