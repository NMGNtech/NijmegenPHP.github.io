Next Meet-Up: Kickoff and TDD!

We've been talking about it quite a while, so it's time for action! January 14th will be the start of NijmegenPHP.We'll focus on getting to know each other. There will be pizza, drinks and a talk by Bèr Kessels about TDD with PHP.

Timeschedule

  • 18.30h Doors open
  • 19.00h A bite to eat
  • 20.00h Official opening of the first NijmegenPHP Meetup
  • 20.15h Bèr Kessels talks about Test-Driven Development (TDD) with PHP
  • ~.~ A lot of time to get to know everyone and have a drink.

Parking

If you need to park your car, we advise you to go to one of the larger parkings as they are quite cheap after 18.00h:

  • Kelfkensbos (400m, open until 0400h)
  • Mariënburg (250m, open until 00.00h)
  • Keizer Karel (800m, open until 04.00h)
