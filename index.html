It's time to meet up again with your fellow PHP developers from the Nijmegen area. Have a drink, a slice of pizza and a great presentation (TBA)!

Presentation: (Re)discovering SPL by Joshua Thijssen

The Standard PHP Library (SPL) might be one of the most powerful, yet the most unused part of PHP, but you are one of those lucky developers who have discovered it! But now what? The lack of documentation about SPL makes it that a lot users don't really harvest the power that SPL brings. During this presentation I will dive into the numerous iterators, data-structures and interfaces that SPL defines and when and how to implement them in your own projects, but we will talk about the edge-cases as well, as in SPL land things don't always are what they seem..
